ABOUT OUR CHURCH

We envision a model church like the first century church through which Christ Jesus will build his kingdom through signs, wonders and miracles and most importantly, the salvation of souls in multitudes. We believe that the Bible is the revelation of God to man, inspired and Holy Spirit breathed written Word of God, We are called to be His children and live a free life through Christ Jesus. So we welcome you to live a life full of Joy, Happiness, Praise, Faith and Freedom.



OUR STATEMENT OF FAITH

There is only one true God who is the eternal King, Creator and Redeemer of all that is. He is perfectly holy, just, loving and truthful. He has revealed Himself to be eternally self-existent - one being in three persons: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.

The Bible is the inspired and only infallible and authoritative Word of God.
